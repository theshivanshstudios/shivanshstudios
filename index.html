About Shivansh Studios

Storytellers of the great Indian wedding

"Every wedding tells a story unlike any other. We believe the most beautiful photographs are the ones that let you relive the emotion years later."

For over seven years we have been a passion-driven studio, based in Jaipur, offering a full range of professional wedding photography and cinematography to couples across India and beyond. Our aim is simple — to capture your most loved moments authentically, without ever losing their natural emotion. We pair artistic vision with meticulous execution so every glance, every tear, and every celebration is preserved beautifully.
